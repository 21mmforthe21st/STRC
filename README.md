# STRC Retirement Model — Digital Social Security for Gen Z

An interactive dashboard that models what could happen if Gen Z had the choice to allocate their 6.2% Social Security payroll tax to STRC preferred stock instead.

## Live Demo
[View the dashboard →](https://21mmforthe21st.github.io/STRC/)

## What This Models
- Monthly compounding over 45 years (age 22–67) with true record dates and T+1 settlement
- ROC (Return of Capital) tax treatment — dividends are tax-free until cumulative dividends exceed your cost basis, then taxed at capital gains rates
- Linear dividend yield taper from 11.5% → 6.0% over the contribution period
- Side-by-side comparison with Social Security at every claiming age (62, 65, 67, 70)
- Trust fund depletion scenario (2034, per the 2025 SSA Trustees Report)

## Adjustable Inputs
- Starting salary, annual raise, paycheck allocation %
- STRC share price, starting/ending dividend yield, reinvestment %
- Capital gains tax rate (federal + state)
- Average and maximum SS benefit amounts
